About Us

Everyone is running at their own pace

Star Power is a nationwide non-profit organization aimed at providing happiness and improving the cognitive skills of students with special needs. Our primary objective is to inspire our stars to discover the endless wonders of the world, reinforcing the belief that it is never too late to embark on new journeys. Star Powerâ€™s mission is to give our stars the opportunity to engage in experiences they may have never envisioned before. Through our various activities like coding and tennis, we hope to see our stars sharing their ideas and creativity through projects and fun interactions with everything around them.

Why Star Power?

This is why we are unique from others

Experience

Our teachers and coaches are experienced with years of teaching experience. We are selected from our tennis/coding skills on and off the court. All of us have gone through sessions of training that are specifically designed to support the success of our stars.

No Cost

All of our programs are completely free alongside all the equipment that we will provide. Our stars have the wonderful opportunity to participate in our tennis lessons without any cost, as we supply them with high-quality t-shirts and rackets at no expense.

Combo

Tennis and coding are uniquely different but complement each other seamlessly. While inherently distinct, they form a perfect match. Our stars will have the opportunity to code their creativity and shoot their shots both on and off the court
